Nigeria

Nigeria is a country located on the western coast of Africa and is often referred to as tyhe "Giant of Africa". This name comes from the diverse geography and its people. Nigeria has 36 states with Abuja as its Federal Capital Territory and Lagos being its largest city. Nigeria's climate ranges from arid to humid equatorial. Over 500 indigenous languages are spoken in Nigeria, including Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, Fula, Edo, Ibibio Tiv and so on. Nigeria has more than 250 ethnic groups and is the most populous black nation on Earth with about 150 million people. Nigeria's current president is Bola Ahmed Tinubu (GCFR). Nigeria is known to be the largest producer of oil and gas in Africa.
