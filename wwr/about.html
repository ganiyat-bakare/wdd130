History

Rafting, also known as whitewater rafting, has a rich history that dates back thousands of years. However, it wasn't until the mid-19th century that rafting began to gain popularity as a recreational activity. The modern era of rafting as an adventure sport began in the late 1960s and early 1970s, with the development of specialized inflatable rafts and the growing interest in outdoor adventure activities. Today, rafting has evolved into a popular outdoor activity, attracting people from all walks of life who seek adventure, adrenaline, and a deep connection with nature.

Rafting destinations can be found in various parts of the world, offering a range of experiences from tranquil float trips to adrenaline-fueled whitewater adventures.
