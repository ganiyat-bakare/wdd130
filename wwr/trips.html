Our Thrill Trips

Half-Day Trips

Our half-day trips are perfected for beginners and those short on time. It is a 2-3 hours adventures through mild to moderate rapids, providing a taste of the excitement and thrill of white water rafting without committing to a full-day journey. If you are the busy type, this trip is just for you!

Full-Day Trips

Our comprehensive experience trip starting in the morning and lasting until late afternoon, navigating various rapids with breaks for lunch and exploration. Participants can expect a full day of excitement, adventure, and the chance to challenge themselves on more intense rapids.

Extreme Rafting Trips

The Extreme rafting trips is channeled through advanced courses with turbulent currents and technical challenges for experienced rafters seeking an intense adrenaline rush on the most challenging rapids.

Private Trips

We tailored our private adventures to cater for specific preferences, group sizes, and customization requests for personalized and exclusive white water rafting experiences.
